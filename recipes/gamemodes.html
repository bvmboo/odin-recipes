Game Modes

In Rocket League, you can play in different modes which will have a different amount of players, or add special mutators to the game such as permanent spike upgrade, for example.

List of game modes

Casual

  • Limited Mode (optional)
  • Duel (1v1)
  • Duo (2v2)
  • Standard (3v3)
  • Chaos (4v4)

Competitive

  • Duel (1v1)
  • Duo (2v2)
  • Standard (3v3)

Sports(Extras)

  • Hoops (2v2)
  • Dropshot (3v3)
  • Rumble (3v3)
  • Snow Day(3v3)

Others

  • Training
  • Custom (Private/Season/Workshop)
