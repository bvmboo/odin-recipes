Welcome to Rocket League

Rocket League is a hybrid between football and cars. It has been around since ~2014 and has seen its popularity increase as the game became Free to Play after being bought by Epic Games. It involves two teams varying from 1 to 4, with a ball and two goals within an arena. Each team tries to best the other in a 5 minutes match.
